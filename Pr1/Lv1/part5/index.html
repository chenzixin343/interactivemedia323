Boats Sail
    On The Rivers
        Boats sail on
            the rivers,And
                ships sail on
                    the seas;But
                        clouds that
                            sail across
                                the sky,Are
                                    prettier far
                                        than these.
                                            There are
                                                bridges on
                                                    the rivers,
                                                        As pretty
                                                            as you please;
